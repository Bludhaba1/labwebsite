Principal Investigator

Dr. Shanti Swaroop Srivastava

Assistant Professor at IISER Berhampur
To understand how cells work and what goes wrong in diseases, we study how proteins interact with each other and other molecules. By using methods like cryo-EM and X-ray crystallography, we uncover detailed structures that can help develop better treatments. These insights allow us to target molecular interactions disrupted in diseases for more effective therapeutic approaches.
  • Single particle cryo-electron microscopy
  • X-ray crystallography
  • Drug discovery
